Privacy Policy
Effective Date: October 23, 2024

At keanoserver.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following information:

IP Addresses: We collect IP addresses from visitors for analytics purposes.
Analytics Data: We use this information to better understand how visitors interact with our website.

2. How We Collect Information
Automatically: When you visit our website, your IP address is automatically collected as part of standard web traffic monitoring.
Through Analytics Services: We use analytics services (such as GitHub and Cloudflare) to monitor website traffic and performance. These services may collect and store your IP address as part of their operations.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:

Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
Improve the website’s performance and content delivery
Ensure the website’s security and prevent fraud

4. How We Protect Your Information
We take the security of your information seriously and implement the following measures:

GitHub Security: Data stored within our website's code repository on GitHub is protected by industry-standard security measures.
Cloudflare: Our website is protected by Cloudflare, which provides additional security features such as DDoS protection, SSL encryption, and web application firewalls to ensure the security of your data.
Squarespace: Our domain is managed through Squarespace, which implements secure DNS management and provides additional layers of security.

5. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data (GDPR)
Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

Legitimate Interests: Collecting IP addresses for the purpose of improving website performance and ensuring security.
Consent: By using our website, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal data as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Sharing Your Information
We do not share your personal information with third parties, except for the following:

Analytics Providers: We may share IP addresses with third-party services like Cloudflare and GitHub for the purpose of website traffic analysis and performance monitoring.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

7. Your Rights Under GDPR
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access: You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
Correction: You may request that we correct any personal data that is inaccurate or incomplete.
Deletion (Right to be Forgotten): You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we may have.
Data Portability: You have the right to request the transfer of your data to another service.
Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent to data collection at any time, though this may affect your ability to use our website.

8. Cookies
Our website uses cookies for analytics purposes. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device to help us improve your experience on our website.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

10. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@keanoserver.com.
